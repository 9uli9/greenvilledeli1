About Us

Hey there, food enthusiasts! Ready to embark on a taste adventure? Greenville Deli, nestled right in the heart of the city, is your go-to spot for a laid-back yet flavorsome dining experience.

Our culinary wizards are like food artists, crafting each dish with a mix of love and local goodness. Picture this: gourmet breakfasts that make mornings better and lunches that'll have you doing the happy food dance.

We're all about fresh, locally sourced ingredients – think crispy bacon, farm-fresh eggs, and the juiciest tomatoes. Our menu is your ticket to a flavor symphony, carefully orchestrated to please even the pickiest taste buds.

Breakfast
At Greenville Deli, our wonderful breakfast menu invites you to savor the perfect blend of flavors, ensuring each bite is a delightful start to your day. Join us, where every morning becomes a celebration of delicious moments!

Lunch

Embark on a flavorful journey with our exquisite lunch menu at Greenville Deli, where each dish is a crafted masterpiece, promising a delightful midday indulgence that satisfies both the palate and the soul!
